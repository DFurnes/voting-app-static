FAQ

What is Celebs Gone Good?

Celebs Gone Good is an annual list honoring celebrities who use their fame and influence to create positive change.

How long has Celebs Gone Good been around?

This is the ninth year DoSomething.org has powered Celebs Gone Good.

What’s the difference among the three categories?

Celebs Gone Good: These stars are all over phones, TVs, playlists, and movie screens. They're dominating the headlines -- and dominating social change.

Celebs to Watch: These up-and-coming celebs have bright futures both in terms of star power and social good.

Internet Celebs: These influencers use the power of their online platforms to make a serious impact.

How often can I vote?

Once a day and one vote per category!

When does voting end?

Saturday, December 24, 2016 at 11:59 pm EST.

When will the winners be announced?

Tuesday, December 27, 2016.

How are the votes decided?

The winners of Celebs Good Good, Celebs to Watch, and Internet Celebs are selected based on three criteria: 1) Votes from the public, 2) STARmeter ranking on the Internet Movie Database, and 3) Input from industry experts.

Who should I contact for press inquiries?

Have questions? Want more info? Email Colleen Wormsley at cwormsley@dosomething.org.

What is DoSomething.org?

DoSomething.org is a global movement for good. We’re activating 5.5 million young people (and counting!) to make positive change, both online and off. And it’s already happening in every area code in the US and in over 131 countries!

When you take action with DoSomething.org, you join something bigger than yourself. You team up with the young people who’ve run the largest sports-equipment drive. And clothed over half of America’s teens in homeless shelters. And cleaned up 3.7 million(!) cigarette butts around the world. You’ve got the power and the passion to make a difference on any issue you want -- we’ll help you get it done. Welcome to DoSomething.org. Let’s Do This.
