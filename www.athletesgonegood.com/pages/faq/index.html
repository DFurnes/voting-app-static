FAQ

What is Athletes Gone Good?

Athletes Gone Good was created by DoSomething.org, the largest organization for young people and social change, to celebrate athletes who are using their influence for good.

Athletes Gone Good was inspired by the success of Celebs Gone Good, an annual list DoSomething.org created for the past 7 years.

How many times can I vote?

Now through July 31st, you can vote for your favorite athlete once per day until Friday, July 31st at 11:59 pm EST.

How can I submit a nomination request for an athlete?

If you don’t see your favorite athlete, submit them to be nominated here.

How are winners selected?

The winners of Athletes Gone Good are compiled as a result of a three-part methodology (1) Votes and nominations from the public (2) Athlete’s influence on social media and (3) Advice from the Athletes Gone Good Advisory Committee.

The final winners will be announced the second week in August.

What is DoSomething.org?
